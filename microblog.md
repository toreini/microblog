### Citizenship
*2025-11-06*

The distinction between humanship and citizenship is occupying my mind recently. It is interesting how modern societies evolve around a concept of nation (and political border). As government, the institution is mandated to protect its citizens, not all the humans living in their countries. The digital world is border-less, yet such concepts are somehow replicated there. GDPR is a good example. Verifiable Credentials are the next to shape the notion of citizenship documents in digital world. I strongly believe having it is better than not, but I think there are many complex trust-building ingredients to resolve. I will talk about them gradually, as my brain dumps get more structured.
